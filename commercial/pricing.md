## FAQ

**Can I use the Free tier in production?**
Yes. The SDK is Apache 2.0 licensed. Use it anywhere, no strings attached.

**What happens when my trial ends?**
Your data is preserved for 30 days. Upgrade anytime to pick up where you left off.

**Do you offer annual discounts?**
Yes — 20% off for annual billing on Pro and Team plans.

**Is my data secure?**
All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). Enterprise customers can deploy on their own infrastructure.

**Can I switch plans anytime?**
Yes. Upgrades are immediate. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.
